## Functionality Overview

### Emotion Tracking
- Pick a mood from emojis or custom entry  
- Add personal notes about your day  
- View past moods in calendar or list view  

### Analytics
- Track trends over days, weeks, or months  
- Discover your most frequent moods  
- Visual charts to help reflect on emotional patterns  

### Login & Security
- Email/password authentication  
- Social login with Google and Facebook  
- Real-time error handling
